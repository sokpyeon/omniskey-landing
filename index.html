OMNIS KEY executes RUNTIME

Local agent runtime on your machine. Spawns agents, manages sessions, handles tools and channels. The execution layer everything runs on.

COSMIC reasons INTELLIGENCE

10-engine probabilistic reasoning pipeline. Not autocomplete — causal inference, temporal modeling, adversarial stress testing, and confidence scoring on every output.

HEIMDALL verifies TRUST

SHA-256 attestation chain on every output. Source tiering (T1/T2/T3), trust scores 0-100, provenance manifests. Nothing ships unverified.
